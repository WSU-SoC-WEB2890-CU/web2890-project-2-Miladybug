7 Things Inklings and Geeklings Can do for You

Welcome! I am so excited to be starting this chapter of my life, and with it, this blog. I have big ideas, big plans, and big dreams, and all of them, dear reader, involve helping you. I'm starting this blog with the goal of spreading hope, creativity, and joy. I want to help you tell your story, embrace your divine ability to create, and bless your family while doing it. How? Well, I'm glad you asked! Here are seven things you can expect me to do for you if you stick around.

  1. Review, vet, and suggest some incredible books

    Books. They're what I do. They're what I LIVE for. If I could read for a living, I would. Oh wait, I do! Really though, I devour books. I read as much as I can, as often as I can, and as thoroughly as I can. And from that, I get some pretty good suggestions. However, I know that not everyone has the same taste when it comes to books, so I'm committed to giving you detailed reviews and specific recommendations. I'll provide content and trigger warnings, specify what audience a book is meant for, and give you an idea of what you'll enjoy based on what you've already read. Keep an eye out for round up lists, reviews, and teasers! I also LOVE delving into the depth of a story, so you may even see an analysis here and there. I read a lot of clean romance, YA, and classics, but you'll also see some recommendations from my little Geeklings!

  2. Give some fabulous writing and editing advice

    You may have gathered already that I'm a little obsessed with books. You know, maybe. I mean, I did get my degree in English, with an emphasis in creative writing, and I currently work as an editor. I may not have written the next great American novel yet, but I know a few things! And I'm happy to share what I've learned, as well as what I'm still learning! Watch for tips and tricks, grammar explained, and vital editing and publishing info. I'm here to help!

  3. Connect you to the perfect editorial and publishing services

    It's me. Hi! I'm the editor! It's me. Okay, maybe not for every stage of your publishing journey, but I do offer some pretty great editorial services. If you need line editing, copyediting, or proofreading, I'm your girl! I do specifically stick to clean/wholesome manuscripts, but if that's you, I'd love to chat! Check out my services page and send me an email if I can help you! And for the services I don't provide, I'll help connect you with people who can do exactly what you need. I'll be featuring posts about cover design, beta reading, developmental and content editing, and marketing. If you offer one of these services, let me know and I'll be glad to feature you! And if you're looking for someone, I'll do my best to help you find the right professional for the job.

  4. Teach and provide ideas for a variety of crafts and creative pursuits

    Although I primarily center around books and writing here, the real goal is to grow overall creativity. I'm all about finding ways to express yourself through whichever medium call to you. I love trying new things, so you'll see a variety of crafting ideas on here. I'll most likely feature a lot of crochet, sewing, Cricut, and bullet journaling, but I'm always on the lookout for fun new ideas, and new artistic practices to try. I believe that if you want to, you should! Even if you don't commit to becoming a master, arts and crafts are a beautiful form of creative self care.

  5. Help you organize your ideas and goals

    If you're anything like me, and you want to try EVERYTHING, it can be hard to know where to start and what to focus on. Do you have a million projects going at once? Do you have a hard time finishing anything? Cause same. But we can work on this together! I'll be sharing a lot of my bullet journaling ideas and methods, and that's helped me a lot! I'll also provide tips for sticking with a project, organizing your mind and your space, and setting reasonable creative goals.

  6. Guide you as you teach your children to live a life of literacy, creativity, and joy

    The only calling I feel more deeply than creativity is that of motherhood, and I know I'm not alone. My kids are my world! And there's so much I want to teach them about stories and art and love and FUN! But it can be hard to take time to love yourself when you're so focused on loving little Geeklings. So I'm going to help you do both. Keep an eye out for educational, sensory, and fun ideas for things to do with your littles, as well as tips for surviving motherhood, whether you're a stay at home mom, a working mom, a single mom, or just a hot mess mom.

  7. Provide a space of love, understanding, and creative freedom

    It can be a lonely world out there. It can be hard to make friends as an adult, and especially a mother. It can be hard to live authentically when there's so much judgement around you. I see you. I hear you. You're doing great, and you are enough. I want to make sure you remember that! Check in for an occasional pep talk, reassurance, and the certainty that this is a safe space, and I am a safe person. If you're looking for specific resources, tips, or ideas, let me know, and I'll see what I can do! I hope this will turn into a fruitful business for me, but mostly, I just want to help. I want to help you tell your story, and I want to love you while you do it.

I am so excited to be here. I hope you can feel my sincere joy through my words, because I really believe this is the path I am meant to be on. I can't wait to share my story, and to help you share yours. I can't wait to teach my children and watch them grow in joy, and to help you do the same. My beautiful friends, I hope you'll stick around! Leave a comment letting me know how I can help you, and I'll find the best way for us to learn and grow together.
