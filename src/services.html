Services

Line Editing

($0.03-0.04/word)

Line editing takes place before copyediting and after developmental editing. During this phase, I will examine your manuscript sentence-by-sentence, leaving suggestions on word choice, tone, and cohesion. A line edit does not focus on grammar, but on readability.

Copyediting

($0.02-0.03/word)

Copyediting takes place before formatting. A copyedit focuses on grammar, clarity, syntax, and consistency, as well as ensuring the manuscript follows the chosen style guide (typically Chicago Manual of Style). I may do some fact checking, but I am not responsible for checking all the research. Copyediting is my specialty!

Proofreading

($0.015-0.02/word)

Proofreading is the final review before a manuscript goes to print. This takes place after the book has been formatted. I will look for minor grammar and punctuation mistakes, as well as formatting errors.
